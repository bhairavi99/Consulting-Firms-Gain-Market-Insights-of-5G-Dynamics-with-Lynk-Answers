Consulting Firms Gain Market Insights of 5G Dynamics with Lynk Answers

By Lynk
16 SEP 2022

The 5G market worldwide is projected to reach a valuation of US$1.67 trillion by 2030, with a 52 percent CAGR growth from 2022-30. However, merely 19 percent of US business professionals claim that they understand the benefits of 5G. How to reap benefits from 5G and understand its related challenges, outlook and use cases have become key. Therefore, researching and consulting firms should play a role in bridging the gap by carrying out market research and talking to industry experts to help organizations identify 5G applications and come up with tailored solutions. 

Challenge

The executive researcher of a consulting firm in East Asia wanted to understand the global competitive landscape for 5G technology and new business opportunities, for their market research. 

Key Questions:

  • What are the new business opportunities and use cases of 5G? 
  • What is the impact of 5G on automation?

Outcome Gained: 

  1. To achieve desired insights and objectives, the firm consulted key experts on Lynk Answers from leading telecommunications and consumer electronics businesses including a CTO (Southeast Asia), a Chief Engineer (North America) and a Head of Strategy (Europe).
  2. The firm gathered on-the-ground insights within 2 weeks of the request through 1-on-1 consultations, which would usually take a local market research agency 6–8 weeks.
  3. The firm is able to access the transcript of consultations to look up key insights from the conversation later on. 
  4. Through interactions with experts in 5G, the consulting firm gained a real-time in-depth understanding of the 5G proliferation, and expansion of 5G verticals into artificial and virtual realities. 
  5. They understood the 5G market size dynamics, factors directing growth, and segmentation of the 5G sub-sectors. They aligned the 5G use cases into their strategies for enhancing business networks. 

The researchers were able to quickly narrow down to experts from over 100 experienced telecommunication professionals accessible via Lynk Answers. This immensely sped up their research processes, filled in the inadequacies and gained relevant insights to support their analyses used to direct their company’s business strategy.
